# Step 5: Who Reviews

Every PR is reviewed by **four mandatory teams**:

| Review Team              | Role                                             |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Architecture**         | Final decision authority, drives resolution      |
| **Product Architecture** | Cross-component alignment & product sign-off     |
| **VTS Team**             | Validation & test strategy review                |
| RTAB Group               | System-level alignment & stakeholder visibility  |

Plus a **domain-specific MW team reviewer** based on the component:

**AV Architecture** | **Broadcast Team** | **Control Manager Architecture** | **Graphics Architecture** | **Connectivity Architecture** | **Kernel Architecture**
