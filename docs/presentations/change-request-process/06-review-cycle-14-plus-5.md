# Step 4: The 14+5 Review Cycle

## Phase A: 14-Day Open Review Window

- PR is published and assigned to **all mandatory and domain reviewers**
- All technical feedback must be submitted within **14 calendar days**
- Review progress tracked in each component's `metadata.yaml`
- **Silence equals consent** - reviewers who do not respond are deemed to have no objections

## Phase B: 5-Business-Day Resolution Sprint

| Day   | Activity                                                  |
|-------|-----------------------------------------------------------|
| 1-3   | **Implementation** - HAL Lead triages and addresses feedback |
| 4     | **Sanity Check** - 24-hour verification, no new features  |
| 5     | **The Merge** - Status updated to GREEN, merged to develop |
